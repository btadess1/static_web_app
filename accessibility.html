Accessibility Statement

Accessibility

Habesha Place always strives to make its online and in person services accessible to a widest possible customers or users, by following Web Content Accessibility Guidelines.

To improve our accessibility, we have tried to follow the following design guidelines:
We have applied semantic HTML tags appropriately.
We have used external Cascading Style Sheet to control our pages layout.
We have provide alternative texts for all images and graphics.
We have also added additional markup in our forms to enhance accessibility, and to help
assistive technologies.

Website Accessibility Questions

You may contact us, for any website accessibility questions. Since we are always engaged in a continuous process
of improvement, we always welcome any constructive input to improve our website accessibility. Should you face
any accessibility issues, please Contact Us
