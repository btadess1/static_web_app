About Us

HabeshaPlace organizes Taraditional wedding cerrimonies, funneral Services, and political and religious conferences to the Ethiopian and Eretrian communities in the Washington metropolitant area.In addition, we organize trainings for drivers. We also give English language training, and database training courses.

We are dedicated to organizing high quality wedding ceremonies through unparalleled Ethiopian and Eretrean traditional foods, musics, and decoration.


Based in Silver Spring, MD, in the center of the downtown, where thoudands of Habesha people live, HabeshaPlace organizes more than 30 weddings and number of other events annually accross the metropolitant area. For our story and founding members information, read the article below.
